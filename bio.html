About Scandal Murty: The Person & The Analyst

The Story Behind the Name

Scandal. Yeah, that's my real name, and here is where it came from: My mom was a big fan of the 1993 TV show *Cobra*. She loved it so much that she named me after the main character, who was also named Scandal. It's a great story, and it definitely makes for a good laugh. After all, what's life without a little bit of a unique start?

My Approach to Data

As an aspiring **Data Analyst / Business Analyst**, I've found my passion in translating complex data into **clear, actionable insights**. My motivation stems from the belief that behind every dataset is a story waiting to be told—a story that can drive efficiency and growth. My **Associate's degree in Data Analytics** has equipped me with a strong technical foundation in **SQL, Python, and Tableau**, which I leverage to solve problems both professionally and in my personal projects.

I thrive on connecting the technical details of data science with the strategic needs of a business, making me eager to contribute fresh perspectives and methodologies to a forward-thinking team.
